Old Hungarian script (Rovás Irás) was the Hungarian script used primarily before the year 1000, before the christianisation of Hungary. Due to Hungary converting to Roman Catholicism, it lead to the language being Latinised and the removal of the old 'pagan' writing system. Old Hungarian is read right to left, likely due to people carving into wood/stone and this being easier and safer right to left as opposed to most languages being left to right. This page's purpose is to teach Hungarian runic writing to everyone, so that we Hungarians can use it once again, because our ancestors created this script, thus we must respect our past.

Jegyzetek: Nincsen Dz, Dzs, Q, W, X, Y. Ezek a betűk csak később lett a magyar ábécébe. Mai korabeli emberek csináltak a betűk erre de ez nem a volt eredeti rovás irás.

Notes: There is no Dz, Dzs, Q, W, X, Y. These letters were only implemented later in the Hungarian alphabet. There were contemporary ligatures made for these letters but this was not the original Hungarian Runic writing.

𐲥𐲋𐲓𐲉𐲗 𐲏𐲐𐲘𐲙𐲪𐲥

(Székely Himnusz)

𐲓𐳐 𐳦𐳪𐳇𐳒𐳀 𐳘𐳉𐳢𐳢𐳉⹁‏ 𐳘𐳉𐳢𐳢𐳉 𐳮𐳐𐳥 𐳀 𐳮𐳋𐳍𐳯𐳉𐳦

𐲘𐳁𐳢 𐳘𐳁𐳤𐳌𐳋𐳖𐳉𐳯𐳉𐳢 𐳋𐳮 𐳜𐳦𐳀 𐲆𐳀𐳂𐳀 𐳙𐳋𐳠𐳉
