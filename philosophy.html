My philosophy

How much I follow this "philosophy" is like any other philosophy, it's easy to call yourself a big fan of stoicism and it's ideas but how do you act when suddenly you are broke, your girlfriend leaves you (if you even had one lol) and your dog just died. Outside of this, I myself am a fan of the movements of ancient stoicism, producing > consuming, and action is greater than knowledge.
